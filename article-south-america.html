Alfaror

It is impossible to leave Argentina without having tasted the famous alfajor, a small pastry that it is impossible to resist! These are small round cookies filled mostly with dulce de leche and topped with dark, white or milk chocolate. On the other hand, if you want to taste real artisanal alfajores, it is good to know that the world of alfajores is divided into two: industrialists and gourmets, from artisanal recipes.

Colaciones Cordobesas

These pastries are typical of the north-central region of Argentina. Córdoba is the second largest city in the country. It is a university town and very touristy. The colaciones have the same origin as the famous alfajores. They are prepared in an artisanal way and they are sold in regional product stores and also in the street.

Pan Dulce

Pan dulce is a sweet bread from Latin America. It is served at Christmas.
