Travel along my passion, PASTRY

Some people travel for the beauty of the landscapes, for the discovery of nature, of animals. Others will travel to enjoy the sun, go to the mountains, chill at the seaside ... My website invites you to travel differently through the pastries photographys and thus discover local specialties, culture, food, customs of a continent, a region or a country. I want to invite you to travel through my world pastry photos and make you want to travel the world with me.
I share my photos of my favorite pastries encountered during trips and my meetings with exceptional pastry chefs. If you are a pastry chef, contact me for photographer services to give visibility to your talents.
So follow me through the world and enjoy pastries photography !

I am Lane, Photographer

From lamingtons (Australia) to makroud (Marrocco)

My name is Lane MIS and I had my first camera when I was 14 years old. I bought it in a flea market in the suburbs of Melbourne where I come from. I started by taking pictures of everything that brought me happiness in my life. And it is naturally, during a student job in a French pastry shop in Melbourne that I associated my two favourites passions. At the end of my microbiology studies, I had the chance to travel alone around the world. I discovered so many new pastries in each country and region that I decided to combine my trip and pastry! Today I share my favorites and explain to you how to according travel to your favorite pastries!
Now I devote myself to the development of my work as a photographer specializing in pastries. You can also follow me on social networks to see my latest photos !
