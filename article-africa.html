Koeksisters

A koeksister, koeksuster or koesister is a South African pastry with braided donut dough and coated with syrup. The dough is fried like donuts, then soaked in a bath of cold cane syrup. Koeksisters are eaten cold, are very sticky and sweet. Their taste is similar to honey.
